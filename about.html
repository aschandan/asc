Who am I?

I'm Amandeep Chandan, a student at Wake Technical Community College. I am currently studying graphic design. While in high school, I earned certifications ranging from Adobe products to Microsoft products.

What do I do?

I aim to create work that solves problems and excites. I also aim for attention to detail that'll leave my eyes and head hurting from the nitpicking.

While I always attempt to seek for perfection within my work, I may not always be able to give it my all. During these times I always hope to learn from these mistakes and provide something of quality for others and myself.

Not always thinking about work

In my free time, I've been working on a menagerie of things to help improve myself and to have fun in this world. Things such as journaling, reading, learning Norwegian, and writing. Also in my spare time I tinker with this site in hopes of learning how fun web design can be.
